Eggs on Toast Recipe

Delicious Eggs on Toast

Yummy 4 ingredient eggs on toast that is extremely easy to cook

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 Slices of Toast
  • Butter
  • Dash of Milk

Steps

  • Crack eggs into bowl, add dash of milk and beat
  • Put in hot pan over stove and stir until cooked
  • Toast bread and butter it
  • Eat!!
