Delicious Banana Porridge

Yummy 4 ingredient porridge that is extremely easy to cook

Ingredients

  • 100g Oats
  • 40g Peanut Butter
  • 250ml Almond Milk
  • 1 Banana

Steps

  • Put oats and almond milk in a pot and cook on stovetop
  • Once oats are creamy texture add peanut butter and banana
  • Mix and serve
  • Eat!!
