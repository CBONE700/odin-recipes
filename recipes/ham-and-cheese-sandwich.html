Ham and Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Delicious Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Yummy 4 Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 3 Slices of Ham
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 1 Slice of Cheese
  • Mustard

Steps

  • Put ham and cheese on slice of bread
  • Put mustard on top of cheese
  • Close sandwich
  • Eat!!
